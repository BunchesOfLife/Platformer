Platforming 101
by Brady Murren
v 1.0.2

Description:
Randomly generates a level out of a file folder of level 
block images.  Choose how many level blocks you want.
Will not generate more than one of the same block.  
Health is in top left corner of playing screen.  
Reach the pink circle at end to win!

Controls:
-Use up/down arrow keys to choose amount of level blocks.
-Press P when playing to pause and see controls.
-Press Escape at any time to close game.
